Grilled Cheese Recipe

Back to Home Page

This delicious recipe will guide you into creating an amazing grilled cheese sandwich
as shown in the image above.

Ingredients

  • Bread: 2 slices (preferably 1/2-inch thick sourdough or white sandwich bread)
  • Butter: 2 teaspoons (softened)
  • Cheese: 1 to 2 ounces (grated cheddar, Swiss, or Gruy√®re, depending on the size of your bread)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Preheat the Skillet: Place a heavy-bottomed skillet over medium-low heat.
  2. Butter the Bread: Spread 1 teaspoon of softened butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Add the Cheese: Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the skillet. Sprinkle the grated cheese evenly over the bread.
  4. Top with the Other Slice: Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side up.
  5. Cook the Sandwich: Cover the skillet and cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and crisp, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip the sandwich, replace the lid, and cook until the other slice of bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, 1 to 3 minutes. Reduce the heat to low if the bread is getting too brown before the cheese is fully melted.
  6. Serve: Remove the sandwich from the skillet, slice in half diagonally, and serve immediately.
