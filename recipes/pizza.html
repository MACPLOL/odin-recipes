Pizza Recipe

Back to Home Page

This delicious recipe will guide you into creating a mouth-watering pizza pie
as shown in the image above.

Ingredients

  • Active Dry Yeast: 1 1/4-oz packet (2 1/4 teaspoons)
  • Water: 1 1/2 cups (90 to 100°F)
  • All-Purpose Flour: 4 cups (500g)
  • Olive Oil: 2 tablespoons, plus more for brushing
  • Kosher Salt: 2 1/4 teaspoons
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese: 1/4 cup (divided)
  • Homemade Pizza Sauce: 1 cup (divided)
  • Low-Moisture Mozzarella Cheese: 3 cups (divided)
  • Cubed Fresh Mozzarella Cheese: 1 cup (divided)
  • Fresh Basil: for serving

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, mix yeast and sugar with warm water. Let sit until bubbles form, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until dough comes together, then increase speed to medium-low until dough is sticky, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Drizzle the top of the dough with remaining olive oil, flip to coat, cover, and let rise for 1 hour and 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C) with a pizza stone on the upper third rack.
  5. Cut dough in half, form into rounds, and let rise on a baking sheet.
  6. On a floured surface, press one round into an 8-inch circle, then stretch to a 12-inch circle.
  7. Place dough on a piece of heavy-duty foil on an upside-down half-sheet pan, brush with olive oil, and dust with flour.
  8. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan, spread 1/2 cup of pizza sauce, and add 1 1/2 cups of low-moisture mozzarella and 1/2 cup of cubed fresh mozzarella.
  9. Transfer pizza with foil to the preheated baking stone, and bake until crust is browned and cheese is bubbling, about 12-14 minutes.
  10. Brush the crust with olive oil, sprinkle with basil, and serve hot.
